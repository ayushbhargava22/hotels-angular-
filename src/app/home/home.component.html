El Llorenc philosophy

In the heart of the emblematic La Calatrava district, right in the centre of Palma de Mallorca, nestles a truly outstanding hotel, where beauty and comfort converge to offer a one-of-a-kind experience.

An exclusive and welcoming 33 room establishment designed to surprise and delight all its guests. El Llorenç is more than just a hotel. Indeed, it embodies a perfect host, eager to cater to your every need and make you feel truly special. The quintessential city hotel offering a full range of services tailored to meet guests’ needs. A vision based on meeting individual requirements and doing its utmost to provide myriad alternatives, from action-packed activities to total rest and relaxation.

At El Llorenç our aim is to show you the very best Majorca has to offer, making you feel part of the island. Allow us to take you on a journey in time through the streets that line La Calatrava, a modern-day cultural melting pot but also the birthplace of the authentic origin of our island.

We want to surprise and delight you, providing you with a stay that far exceeds your expectations and wishes.

La Calatrava - Palma

Much of this district is built on the site of Palma’s ancient walls, which the locals call Dalt Murada. It is renowned for its unique layout, crisscrossed by the narrow streets of the ancient Moorish city and El Call (the former Jewish quarter). Its striking layout, bustling narrow streets, colours and the wonderful light that floods every corner, together with the buildings and historic palaces all bring this marvellous area and its unique character to life.

Rooftop

The terrace boasts the city’s longest infinity pool, part of a multipurpose space designed for leisure and relaxation. Other facilities include a cocktail and gourmet snack bar, as well as a sun terrace furnished with comfortable sunbeds and Balinese beds providing shade from the sun.

DINS Santi Taura

With the opening of El Llorenç, DINS will be moving from the former carpenter’s workshop in Lloseta, where operations first began, to new premises in the heart of Palma’s La Calatrava district, an authentic cultural melting pot that has influenced recipes that have experienced an authentic revival under the skill and know-how of Santi Taura.
